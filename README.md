# Customer-Behavior-analysis
End-to-end customer shopping behavior analysis using Python, Pandas, and Power BI. Key business insights were communicated through interactive and informative visualizations, transforming raw customer data into actionable recommendations for decision-making. 

## Objectives

The project aims to answer key business questions related to:

* Revenue generation across customer demographics
* Customer spending patterns
* Impact of discounts on purchases
* Subscription behavior and customer loyalty
* Product performance and customer satisfaction
* Revenue contribution by different age groups

## Tools & Technologies

* Python

  * Pandas
* Power BI
* CSV Dataset

## Data Preparation

The dataset was cleaned and prepared using Python by:

* Handling missing values
* Removing duplicate records
* Standardizing column names
* Validating data consistency
* Exporting a cleaned dataset for visualization

## Business Questions Addressed

1. What is the total revenue generated by male and female customers?
2. Which customers used discounts and still spent above the average purchase amount?
3. What are the top-rated products based on customer reviews?
4. How do purchase amounts differ between shipping methods?
5. How do subscribers compare to non-subscribers in terms of spending and revenue?
6. Which products have the highest percentage of discounted purchases?
7. How can customers be segmented into New, Returning, and Loyal groups?
8. What are the most purchased products within each category?
9. Are repeat buyers more likely to subscribe?
10. Which age groups contribute the most revenue?
